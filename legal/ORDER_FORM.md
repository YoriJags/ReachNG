## 5. Authorisations the Client grants

By signing below the Client authorises ReachNG to:

1. Process inbound WhatsApp messages on the paired number for the purpose of drafting reply messages.
2. Draft and queue outbound messages for the Client's approval. **No message is sent without the Client's explicit tap.**
3. Pass message content to the sub-processors listed in the DPA (Anthropic, Unipile, MongoDB Atlas, Railway, Paystack, OpenAI Whisper, PostHog) solely for the purpose of providing the Service.
4. Process voice-note audio (deleted within 24 hours of transcription) and receipt screenshots (deleted within 24 hours of OCR extraction).
5. Persist metadata (timestamps, classifier verdicts, queue state) for up to 13 months for usage measurement and quality scoring.
