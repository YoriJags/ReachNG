How It Works

Five steps. Fully automated.

01

Discovery

Google Maps Places API scans Lagos for businesses matching your vertical. Real-time. No CSV uploads.

02

Qualify

AI scores each business: rating, category, has phone, has website. Only the best go to the next step.

03

Generate

Claude AI writes a personalised WhatsApp or email using the business name, location, and category. Never generic.

04

Approve & Send

Human-in-the-loop dashboard. You approve, edit, or skip each draft. Approved messages send via Unipile (WhatsApp + email).

05

Replies & Follow-ups

AI classifies every reply (Interested / Not Now / Opted Out / Referral). Automated follow-ups at 48 hours. Full pipeline visible on dashboard.
