## Vertical: Insurance — Brokers, Agencies, MGAs, Direct Underwriters' SME Channel, HMOs

### HARD TRUTH — Do NOT lead with "we'll get you policies"
A Lagos insurance broker is not enquiry-starved. They are quote-conversion-broken and renewal-leak-broken. Every "how much for my Camry insurance?" WhatsApp answered the next day is a policy bound at the broker next door. Lead with the QUOTE-TO-BIND gap and silent renewal lapse — not lead-gen. Be careful: NAICOM regulates communication; never quote without underwriter confirmation.

### Who you're reaching
SME insurance brokers (NAICOM-licensed), MGAs (Managing General Agents), bancassurance teams, HMO front-line sales, motor-insurance specialists, marine / aviation cargo brokers, oil & gas energy brokers (small-scale), life-insurance agencies, employee-benefits brokers (group health, group life, group personal accident), agricultural-insurance brokers, takaful (Islamic insurance) operators. Lagos VI / Apapa / Ikeja for premium; mainland for retail. Principal brokers, MD/CEOs, heads of retail.

### Their real pain (quantified in ₦, not abstract)
- A typical motor policy: **₦25K–₦300K premium**, broker commission **15–25%** = ₦4K–₦75K. Lose 10 motor enquiries/week = ₦40K–₦750K/week leaked = ₦2M–₦40M/year.
- SME group health: **₦1M–₦40M premium**, commission ₦100K–₦4M. ONE leaked enquiry/quarter = a junior broker's annual salary.
- Marine / cargo: **₦200K–₦5M per cert**. Forwarders need it within hours; whoever responds fastest binds.
- Life / endowment: **₦50K–₦500K annual premium** × multi-year = **₦M+ lifetime value per policy**. Slow nurture = lost annuity stream.
- Renewal leak — 25–40% of policies don't get renewed because the broker forgot to nudge the customer 30 days before expiry. Each lapsed policy = lost commission + lost lifetime relationship.
- Claims comms — when a customer has a fender-bender, they message at 9pm panicking. Slow / cold response = customer doesn't renew next year, posts complaint on Twitter.
- The kicker — broker principals are negotiating with underwriters all day, attending client meetings, dealing with claims-disputed cases. WhatsApp inbound dies on their unread queue. The "junior account officer" they hire can't quote without underwriter sign-off, so the bottleneck never moves.

### Specific signals to lead with (reference one in the opening line — never generic praise)
- **"DM for quote" / "WhatsApp our motor desk"** in IG bio or website — biggest tell.
- **NAICOM licence display, broker number** on website — premium tier, regulated.
- **Underwriter logos** ("we work with AIICO, AXA Mansard, Leadway, Cornerstone") — established, B2B SME inbound.
- **Multi-line offering** (motor + health + life) — coordination chaos in DMs.
- **HMO-tied health-insurance posts** — high-volume retail.
- **Active LinkedIn presence with industry commentary** — drives inbound.
- **Renewal-season posts** ("Renew before expiry, get 10% loyalty discount") — proves they have a renewal-leak pain.

### Anti-signals — skip or soften
- Direct-only underwriters (AIICO, AXA, Leadway head office) — wrong buyer.
- Unregistered insurance touts — regulatory risk.
- One-person agents working off WhatsApp Status — too small.
