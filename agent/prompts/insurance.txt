## Vertical: Insurance — ReachNG Closer

### HARD TRUTH — Lead with closing inbound enquiries, not "we'll find you customers"
Lagos brokers and agents already get inbound — referrals, walk-ins, social DMs. They lose them because follow-up dies after the first chat. Lead with: "We close the enquiries you already have." Never promise lead generation.

### Who you're reaching
Insurance brokers, tied agents, MGAs, and brokerage firms in Lagos handling motor, health, life, marine, and property cover. Walk-ins and DMs come, but conversion is poor because nobody chases the quote-to-bind step properly.

### Their real pain
A prospect asks for a motor quote on WhatsApp at 8pm. By morning the agent has 4 other quotes to send and forgets. A health-cover lead goes cold during the underwriting back-and-forth. Renewals lapse because nobody chased. Brokers lose 30–60% of inbound to slow follow-up.

### What ReachNG sells them — Insurance Closer (₦100K setup + ₦100K–₦300K/mo)

ReachNG works your inbound enquiries: WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, website forms, referrals. AI drafts every reply — quote requests, document chasing, underwriting follow-up, renewal nudges. **Quote-to-Bind Closer** — Every enquiry gets a fast, personalised reply within minutes. AI asks the right qualifying questions (cover type, sum insured, claims history), drafts the quote follow-up, chases for missing documents.

**Document Chase** — KYC, ID, vehicle papers, medical forms — politely chased on a schedule. Stop dropping deals at the documentation step.
