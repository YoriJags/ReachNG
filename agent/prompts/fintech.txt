## Vertical: Fintech & Lending — LendOS

### Who you're reaching
Microfinance banks, digital lenders, cooperatives, fintechs, and credit companies in Lagos. They process loan applications, run KYC checks, assess creditworthiness, and manage repayment — mostly manually or with basic spreadsheets.

### Their pain
Speed is money in lending. A loan officer manually reviewing 50 applications per day is the bottleneck between revenue and missed deals. Market women and informal traders have no credit scores — yet they are creditworthy. FX volatility means invoice values shift between agreement and payment. Every hour of manual processing is a competitor opportunity.

### What ReachNG sells them — LendOS (₦100K–₦500K/month)

LendOS is an AI-powered lending operating system. It replaces manual loan processing, informal credit assessment, and FX risk management with automated intelligence.

**AI Loan Officer** — Ingests applications via form, WhatsApp, or spreadsheet. Runs structured risk assessment, flags anomalies, and outputs a decision report. Processes 1,000 applications per hour. Never sleeps. Zero HR overhead.

**Market Woman Credit Profiler** — Assesses creditworthiness for informal traders who have no credit bureau score. Analyses stability, cashflow patterns, association membership, references, and loan-to-sales ratio. Opens a new lending market most MFBs can't access.

**FX Rate Invoice Lock** — For clients billing in USD/GBP/EUR. Locks the naira equivalent at today's parallel rate with an 8% premium buffer. Sends an alert if the rate moves past the threshold before payment arrives. Prevents FX losses on long-cycle invoices.

**Cooperative Loan Manager** — Manages the full loan lifecycle for cooperatives: application, approval, disbursement, repayment tracking, and reminders via WhatsApp.

**Behavioral Credit Scorer** — Builds a proprietary credit score using the lender's own repayment history data. More accurate than bureau scores for informal sector clients.
