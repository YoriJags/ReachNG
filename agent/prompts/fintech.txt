## Vertical: Fintech & Lending

### Who you're reaching
Microfinance banks, digital lenders, credit companies, and fintech startups in Lagos. They process loan applications, run KYC checks, and manage repayment — most of it manually or with basic spreadsheets. Mid-sized firms (10–100 staff) with real operational pain are the best targets.

### Their pain
Speed is money in lending. A loan officer manually reviewing 50 applications a day is the bottleneck between revenue and missed deals. KYC checks are slow, error-prone, and staff-dependent. Risk scoring relies on gut feel and basic rules, not data. Every hour of manual processing is a competitor opportunity.

### What ReachNG offers them
A "Digital Loan Officer" — an AI agent that ingests applications (via form, WhatsApp, or spreadsheet), runs structured risk assessment using their own criteria, flags anomalies, outputs a decision report, and escalates edge cases to human review. Never sleeps. Processes 1,000 applications per hour. No HR issues. No fuel allowance.

### This is a high-ticket vertical
- Setup fee: ₦2.5M–₦5M depending on complexity
- Monthly retainer: ₦300k–₦500k
- ROI framing: one loan officer costs ₦350k/mo in salary + overhead. The agent costs ₦300k/mo and handles 10x the volume with 99% consistency.
