## Vertical: Fitness & Wellness — Gyms, Studios, Yoga, Pilates, CrossFit, Personal Trainers, Wellness Centres

### HARD TRUTH — Do NOT lead with "we'll get you new members"
A Lagos boutique gym or yoga studio is not lead-starved. They are member-CHURN-broken and inbound-LEAK-broken. Every "how much for monthly?" DM that goes unanswered until tomorrow morning is a member signed up at the studio next door. Lead with the leak + the silent churn — not lead-gen.

### Who you're reaching
Boutique gyms (50–500 members), yoga & pilates studios, CrossFit boxes, F45 / barre / spin / hot-yoga studios, MMA & boxing gyms, swim schools, personal trainers running PT studios, wellness centres (massage / IV / cryo), nutrition coaching practices, postnatal recovery studios. Lagos Lekki / VI / Ikoyi / Victoria Garden City for premium; Surulere / Yaba / Magodo for mid-tier. Owners are usually founder-trainers, sometimes a manager runs front of house.

### Their real pain (quantified in ₦, not abstract)
- A typical Lagos boutique gym monthly = **₦25K–₦80K**, premium = **₦100K–₦300K/month**. Lose 5 prospective members/month to slow DM response = ₦125K–₦1.5M leaked monthly = a trainer's salary.
- Class-pack studios (yoga / pilates / spin): pack of 10 = **₦80K–₦200K**. Walk-ins enquire all day; whoever answers fastest with photos + pricing + schedule wins.
- Personal trainer 1-on-1: **₦8K–₦25K per session**, packs of 12 = **₦100K–₦300K**. Single lost lead = a week's pay.
- Silent churn — members "ghost" instead of cancelling. Studio doesn't notice they're gone until renewal day. **30–40% of churn is recoverable** with a friendly "we miss you" before they fully drop.
- No-show rate on bookable classes: 20–35%. Each empty mat = a slot the waitlisted member would have filled.
- The kicker — owners are trainers first. They're on the floor coaching at 6am, 10am, 5pm, 7pm. WhatsApp/IG goes unanswered for 3-hour stretches. The "front desk" is part-time, junior, and can't qualify or close.

### Specific signals to lead with (reference one in the opening line — never generic praise)
- **"DM for class schedule" / "WhatsApp to book a class"** in IG bio — biggest tell.
- **Active IG with member testimonials, transformation reels, class clips** — heavy inbound the owner-trainer can't service.
- **Posted class schedule with start times** — every empty slot is recoverable revenue.
- **"New cohort starts Monday" / "Bootcamp registration open" announcements** — short enrolment windows, every WhatsApp counts.
- **Multiple specialties** (gym + yoga + nutrition; or boxing + strength) — coordination chaos across coaches.
- **Diaspora returnee mentions** ("welcoming returnees", "December bootcamp") — high-ticket, time-pressured intake.
- **Reviews mentioning slow front-desk response** — public proof of leak.
- **Premium amenities posted** (sauna, ice bath, recovery room) — premium tier, members expect concierge response.

### Anti-signals — skip or soften
- Mass-market chain gyms (Total Sports, etc) with central admin — wrong buyer.
- Outdoor bootcamps with no fixed venue — too small.
- Hotel gym — wrong economics.
- Trainers without a studio brand or Instagram presence — too solo.
