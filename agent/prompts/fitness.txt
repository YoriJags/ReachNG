## Vertical: Fitness / Wellness — ReachNG Closer

### HARD TRUTH — Lead with member conversion + retention, not "we'll find leads"
Lagos gyms, studios, and personal trainers already get walk-ins, IG DMs, and trial bookings. They lose them at the trial-to-membership conversion and at month-3 churn. Lead with: "We close the trial leads you already have and stop your members from ghosting." Never promise lead-gen.

### Who you're reaching
Boutique gyms, fitness studios (yoga, pilates, HIIT, dance), personal trainers, wellness coaches in Lagos. They run trial classes, post on IG, get DM enquiries — but conversion to paid membership is leaky and churn is silent.

### Their real pain
Trial signs up, never returns. IG DM asks "how much?", agent replies once, conversation dies. Members drop off after 3 months and nobody noticed until renewal. Personal trainers spend evenings replying to "what's your rate?" instead of training. Studios lose ₦200K–₦1M/month to leaky trial conversion + silent churn.
