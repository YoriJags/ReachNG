## Vertical: Law Firms & Legal Services — LegalOS

### Who you're reaching
Mid-sized law firms and company secretaries in Lagos — commercial litigation, corporate law, real estate law, and IP practices. Firms with 5–50 lawyers handling large document volumes: contracts, discovery files, due diligence packs, board resolutions, and compliance filings.

### Their pain
Junior associates spend 30–50 hours a week on document review — reading contracts, flagging risk clauses, cross-referencing case files. Court cause lists require daily manual checking. Board resolutions and statutory filings are prepared manually every cycle. NAFDAC and SON compliance is tracked on spreadsheets that fall behind. The senior partners want faster turnaround. The associates want to do real legal work.

### What ReachNG sells them — LegalOS (₦80K–₦300K/month)

LegalOS is an AI-powered operating system for Lagos law firms and corporate secretarial practices. It removes the document burden from associates and automates compliance tracking.

**Digital Associate** — Reads uploaded contracts and case files, extracts key clauses, flags risk language, cross-references precedent, and produces a structured review memo. 100-page document in under 60 seconds. Never fatigued. Consistent output every time.

**Court Cause List Monitor** — Tracks cause lists for all active matters automatically. Flags any matter appearing in the next 7 days and notifies the assigned lawyer. No more missed hearing dates.

**Corporate Governance Tracker** — Tracks statutory filing deadlines, AGM schedules, regulatory submissions, and board meeting requirements for all clients. Sends alerts before deadlines, not after.

**Board Resolution Generator** — Generates draft board resolutions for routine matters — share allotment, director changes, account signatories — in the correct Nigerian Companies Act format.

**Due Diligence Report Generator** — Produces a structured due diligence report from uploaded company documents and public records. Flags missing documentation and red flags.

**Regulatory Filing Calendar** — Tracks NAFDAC, SON, and SEC renewal dates for all clients. Sends automated reminders. No more missed compliance windows.
