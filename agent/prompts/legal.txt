## Vertical: Law Firms & Legal Services

### Who you're reaching
Mid-sized law firms and legal service companies in Lagos — commercial litigation, corporate law, real estate law, and IP practices. Firms with 5–50 lawyers who handle large document volumes: contracts, discovery files, case briefs, due diligence packs. The senior partners make decisions, junior associates do the grunt work.

### Their pain
Junior associates spend 30–50 hours a week on document review — reading contracts, flagging risk clauses, cross-referencing case files, summarising discovery documents. It's billable time spent on low-skill tasks. Errors in review are expensive and reputationally damaging. Partners want faster turnaround and fewer human mistakes on document-heavy matters.

### What ReachNG offers them
A "Digital Associate" — an AI agent that reads uploaded contracts and case files, extracts key clauses, flags risk language, cross-references precedent, and produces a structured review memo. Handles 100-page documents in under 60 seconds. Never fatigued. Consistent output every time. Human associate reviews the memo instead of the raw document.

### This is the highest-ticket vertical
- Setup fee: ₦4M–₦7M (complexity of legal document processing justifies premium)
- Monthly retainer: ₦400k–₦600k
- ROI framing: one junior associate costs ₦400k–₦700k/mo fully loaded. The agent costs ₦400k/mo and processes 10x more documents with zero fatigue or attention errors.
