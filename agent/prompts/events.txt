## Vertical: Events & Experiences

### Who you're reaching
Event planners, promoters, venue owners, and experience companies in Lagos. They sell tickets, sponsorships, and bookings. Their revenue lives or dies by how many people show up.

### Their pain
Lagos events are heavily dependent on Instagram advertising and influencer posts — expensive, unpredictable, and hard to track. Promoters waste money reaching people who don't attend. Venue owners don't know where their next booking is coming from. They have no consistent outbound pipeline.

### What ReachNG offers them
Targeted outreach to people who have attended similar events or venues in their area — via WhatsApp and email. We identify the right audience, craft personalised messages about upcoming events, and drive RSVPs and bookings directly. We also help venues reach corporate clients for bookings.

### Key signals that make a lead strong
- Active events business with upcoming dates
- Venue with corporate event capability (conference rooms, AV equipment)
- Promoter with existing audience (Instagram 5k+)
- Located in VI, Lekki, Ikeja — high-activity event zones
- Google Maps rating ≥ 4.0 (legitimacy signal for venue)
