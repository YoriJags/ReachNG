## Vertical: Events & Experiences — EventOS

### Who you're reaching
Event planners, promoters, venue owners, caterers, and experience companies in Lagos. They coordinate vendors, manage guest lists, track payments, handle aso-ebi orders, and run ticket sales — all over WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

### Their pain
Events have 15–30 vendors per show. Payments come in late or not at all. Aso-ebi orders get mixed up and cause drama two days before the event. Ticket resellers inflate prices and create fraud. All of it is managed manually, creating chaos at exactly the wrong moment.

### What ReachNG sells them — EventOS (₦30K–₦100K/month per planner)

EventOS is an AI-powered event operations system for Lagos planners and promoters. It automates vendor coordination, aso-ebi tracking, budget management, and ticket fraud detection.

**Event Vendor Coordinator** — Manages vendor confirmations, payment milestones, and delivery timelines via WhatsApp automatically. Sends reminders. Flags late vendors to the organiser. One agent replaces three coordination assistants.

**Aso-ebi Coordinator** — Collects fabric orders via WhatsApp, tracks payments per person, assigns reference numbers, sends collection reminders. Organiser gets a clean order sheet — no missed orders, no confusion.

**Ticket Resale Fraud Detector** — Monitors resale channels for inflated or counterfeit tickets linked to your events. Flags suspicious listings and generates takedown evidence.

**Event Budget Tracker** — Tracks spend against budget in real time as vendor invoices and payments come in. Sends an alert before you go over budget, not after.
