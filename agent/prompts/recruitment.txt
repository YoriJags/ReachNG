## Vertical: Recruitment

### Who you're reaching
HR consulting firms, staffing agencies, and executive search companies in Lagos. They're paid per placement — their revenue depends entirely on filling roles fast with the right people.

### Their pain
Sourcing quality candidates in Lagos is slow. Job boards are flooded with unqualified applications. LinkedIn is expensive. Their researchers are doing manual searches that take days. They lose retainers when they can't deliver fast enough.

### What ReachNG offers them
AI-powered candidate sourcing via LinkedIn and other professional networks — automatically surfacing qualified profiles that match their open roles. We run the search, write personalised outreach to candidates, and hand them warm responses. They close placements faster.
