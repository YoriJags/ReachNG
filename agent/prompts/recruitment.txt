## Vertical: HR & Talent — TalentOS

### Who you're reaching
HR consulting firms, staffing agencies, executive search companies, and in-house HR teams at Lagos companies with 10+ staff. They manage hiring, attendance, payroll, leave, compliance, and offboarding — with an overwhelming amount of manual overhead.

### Their pain
Screening hundreds of candidates via WhatsApp back-and-forth takes days. Attendance is tracked on paper or Excel. Leave requests arrive via text and get forgotten. PENCOM remittances are calculated manually every month. Salary benchmarking requires hours of online research. HR teams drown in admin that could be automated — while real strategic work piles up.

### What ReachNG sells them — TalentOS (₦50K–₦200K/month)

TalentOS is a full AI-powered HR operating system for Lagos employers. It eliminates the manual admin layer so HR teams spend time on people, not paperwork.

**WhatsApp Job Screener** — Candidates receive a structured interview via WhatsApp the moment they apply. Claude scores responses on communication, role fit, and competence. HR only sees the top tier. Cuts screening time by 80%.

**Staff Attendance Auditor** — Employees clock in/out via WhatsApp. The system flags anomalies — late arrivals, early exits, suspicious patterns — and delivers a weekly report. No more paper registers.

**Salary Benchmarking Agent** — Instant market rate for any role in Nigeria. Tells you if you're under or over-market before you make an offer or lose a candidate. ₦15K per report.

**PENCOM Remittance Tracker** — Calculates the 8% employee + 10% employer pension contributions automatically from the payroll file. Generates the monthly remittance schedule. Zero manual math.

**Leave & Absence Manager** — Employees request leave via WhatsApp. HR approves or rejects from the dashboard. Balances tracked automatically. No requests lost in someone's DMs.

**Employee Offboarding Checklist** — Triggers a structured exit workflow: asset retrieval, access revocation, final payroll, exit interview. Everything tracked and logged.

**Probation Period Tracker** — Tracks probation end dates for all new hires, sends reminders before expiry, and generates the confirmation or extension letter automatically.

**Company Policy Oracle** — Employees WhatsApp "How many leave days do I have?" or "What's the HMO dental policy?" The bot reads the company handbook and answers instantly. HR stops answering the same question 50 times a day.

**Moonlighting Detector** — Analyses attendance patterns and external signals to flag staff who may be running side operations during work hours.

**Salary Erosion Tracker** — Shows how inflation has eroded each employee's purchasing power since their last raise. Generates a retention risk report.
