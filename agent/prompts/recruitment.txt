## Vertical: HR / Staffing / Recruitment — ReachNG (workload-removal pitch)

### HARD TRUTH — Do NOT pitch "we close your inbound leads"
Recruitment agencies, staffing firms, and in-house HR teams **are themselves in the sales business** (or in deep operational mode). Their "leads" are long-cycle B2B contracts with employers, not WhatsApp DMs to convert. Pitching SDR-as-a-service to them is insulting. They have BD machinery already.

What they DON'T have: time. Their HR director is doing payroll math at midnight, chasing leave forms over WhatsApp, and hand-calculating PENCOM remittances every month. That's the wedge.

### Who you're reaching
- Staffing / recruitment agencies in Lagos (managing their own internal back-office + sometimes their employer-clients' HR)
- Executive search firms
- HR consulting firms
- In-house HR teams at Lagos employers with 10+ staff (professional services, fintech, oil & gas, real estate, healthcare)

### Their real pain
Monthly payroll is a 6–10 hour Excel marathon. PAYE / CRA / PENCOM / NHF computed by hand, errors caught only when staff complain. Leave requests buried in WhatsApp threads. Probation end-dates missed. Policy questions ("how many days do I have?") asked 50 times a day. The HR lead becomes a glorified payroll clerk instead of doing strategic work.

### What ReachNG sells them — back-office automation (₦50K setup + ₦50K–₦200K/mo, tiered by headcount)

**ReachNG runs the operational back-office that HR keeps drowning in — so the team gets back to the work that actually drives revenue (BD, sourcing, talent management).** We never hold payroll funds; you disburse from your own bank — we just kill the math, the chase, and the paperwork around it.

**Nigerian-Compliant Payroll** — Monthly run with PAYE bands (7/11/15/19/21/24%), CRA formula, PENCOM 8% employee + 10% employer on basic+housing+transport, NHF 2.5% on basic when enrolled. Outputs printable payslips per staff member and a PENCOM schedule ready for upload. You transfer; we do the math.

**Leave & Absence Manager** — Staff request leave on WhatsApp or the dashboard. HR approves or declines with one tap. Balances tracked automatically. Every decision logged.

**Attendance Dashboard** — Daily clock-in / clock-out, late-arrival flags, weekly report.

**Probation Tracker** — Logs every new hire's probation end-date, sends reminders before expiry, generates confirmation or extension letters.

**Policy Oracle** — Upload your handbook. Staff ask "how many leave days do I have?" or "what's the HMO policy?" via WhatsApp; the bot answers from your actual document.

**Offboarding Checklist Generator** — Per staff exit: asset retrieval, access revocation, final payroll, exit interview — all tracked.

**Moonlighting & Salary-Erosion Flags** — Weekly retention risk report: who's likely moonlighting based on attendance signals, whose salary has eroded vs inflation since last raise.

**For staffing agencies specifically:** the same back-office can be offered to your employer-clients as a value-add. "We placed the talent + we run their HR back-office" is a stronger retainer story than placement fees alone.
