## Vertical: Real Estate — EstateOS

### Who you're reaching
Property developers, estate agents, landlords, and real estate companies in Lagos with active portfolios. They manage multiple properties, screen tenants, chase rent, conduct viewings, and close deals — almost entirely manually over WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

### Their pain
Landlords chase tenants every month by phone. Agents waste entire days on unqualified viewings. There is no systematic way to score a tenant's affordability before they move in. Legal closing is a last-minute scramble to gather documents. A property manager handling 20 units is drowning in admin they should never be touching.

### What ReachNG sells them — EstateOS (₦80K–₦250K/month)

EstateOS is an AI-powered back office for Lagos real estate businesses. We never hold your money — tenants pay you directly into your own account — we just automate everything *around* the money.

**Rent Collector Loop** — Automated WhatsApp chase for overdue rent, tone escalating across 5 stages from friendly → firm → formal quit notice referencing Lagos Tenancy Law. Every message includes **your** bank details for direct transfer. Dashboard shows who paid, who's overdue, and where each tenant sits on the escalation ladder. Every draft is human-approved before it sends — no AI surprises.

**Neighborhood Scorecard** — For any Lagos address, generates a scorecard showing commute time to VI / Ikoyi / Ikeja at 8AM peak (live Google Maps), nearby amenity count, and a Lagos flood-risk rating. Share it with buyers to close faster.

**KYC Document Vault** — Upload a tenant's NIN slip, international passport, or utility bill — Claude Vision extracts every field, flags suspicious elements, and files it in a searchable vault per tenant. Ready for your lawyer in one click.

**Proof of Funds Screener** — Before you waste a Saturday on a viewing, paste the buyer's bank statement summary into the dashboard. Claude returns PROCEED / REQUEST MORE / DECLINE with a Lagos-specific authenticity rationale (round-number flags, no transaction history, missing letterhead).

**Property Concierge** — A Claude-powered Q&A that reads your property documentation and answers buyer questions via WhatsApp or the dashboard 24/7 ("Is there a C of O?", "What's the service charge?", "Is there a generator?"). Your agents stop answering the same 10 questions 40 times a day.

**Tenant Affordability Score** — Enter a tenant's income, employer, and guarantor info. Get back a LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH risk rating, a rent-to-income ratio, the top risk flags, and a recommendation (APPROVE / APPROVE WITH CONDITIONS / DECLINE). Senior landlord judgment, instant.

**Lawyer Handover Bundle** — When a deal is agreed, the system generates a formal handover memo (parties, price, documents checklist, Land Use Act flags, Governor's Consent status, red flags) and zips the full document bundle for the closing lawyer. Zero scramble.

**Property Inventory Dashboard** — One dashboard for every unit you manage. Track status, list tenants per unit, and see your full portfolio at a glance.
