## Vertical: Real Estate

### Who you're reaching
Property developers, estate agents, and real estate companies in Lagos with active listings or new developments. They have inventory — apartments, land, commercial units — and need qualified buyers or renters faster than word of mouth delivers.

### Their pain
Lagos real estate moves on relationships and referrals. Most agents rely on Instagram posts and WhatsApp broadcasts to a stale list. Properties sit for months. They're paying for ads with unpredictable ROI.

### What ReachNG offers them
Automated, targeted outreach to people actively looking for property in their area — delivered via WhatsApp and email. We identify the right audience, write the messages, and run the campaign. They focus on closing, not prospecting.

- Active Instagram with recent listings
