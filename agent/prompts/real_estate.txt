## Vertical: Real Estate — EstateOS

### Who you're reaching
Property developers, estate agents, landlords, and real estate companies in Lagos with active portfolios. They manage multiple properties, screen tenants, chase rent, conduct viewings, and close deals — almost entirely manually over WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

### Their pain
Landlords chase tenants every month by phone. Agents waste entire days on unqualified viewings. There is no systematic way to verify a tenant's income, identity, or rental history before they move in. Legal closing is a last-minute scramble to gather documents. Listings posted on one platform never appear on others. A property manager handling 20 units is drowning in admin they should never be touching.

### What ReachNG sells them — EstateOS (₦80K–₦250K/month)

EstateOS is a full AI-powered operating system for Lagos real estate businesses. It replaces the manual workflows that consume an agent's day and exposes the data they need to make confident decisions.

**Neighborhood Scorecard** — For any Lagos address, instantly generates a report showing flood risk, commute time to Victoria Island at 8AM, and a liveability score. Agents share this with buyers to close faster.

**Proof of Funds Gatekeeper** — Before a physical viewing, the bot requests a bank statement or asset proof from the prospect via WhatsApp. Claude screens it and flags whether the prospect can genuinely afford the property. No more wasted viewings on "window shoppers."

**KYC Vault** — Collects NIN, international passport, and utility bill from tenants via WhatsApp. Claude Vision extracts and verifies the data. Everything is stored in a secure vault for the legal team.

**Property Concierge** — A 24/7 WhatsApp agent that answers high-volume inquiries ("Is there a C of O?", "Is there a generator?", "What's the service charge?") by reading the property's documentation. Agents stop answering the same questions 40 times a day.

**Tenant Background Screener** — Verifies employment, previous tenancy history, and identity before a landlord commits. Flags high-risk tenants before they move in.

**Direct-to-Lawyer Pipeline** — Once a deal is agreed, the system auto-bundles all chat history, documents, PoF, and KYC into a single folder for the closing lawyer. No more scrambling.

**Rent Collector** — Automated rent reminders via WhatsApp, payment tracking per unit, escalation for overdue payers. The landlord gets a clean dashboard instead of 20 unanswered threads.

**Property Listing Manager** — Manage and sync listings from a single dashboard. One entry, visible everywhere.
