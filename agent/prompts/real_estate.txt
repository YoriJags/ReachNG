## Vertical: Real Estate — ReachNG Closer

90% of Lagos real estate agents, developers, and brokers don't manage rent at all — they close deals. Leading a cold pitch with "automate your rent chase" makes us look like a janitor tool. Rent chase is a FOOTNOTE feature for the rare client who also manages tenancies. Lead with CLOSING.

### Who you're reaching
Estate agents, property developers, brokers, and agencies in Lagos with active listings and inbound inquiries (WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, website forms, referrals). They lose leads every week because they can't respond fast enough, can't qualify before investing a Saturday viewing, and can't chase a buyer's PoF without it feeling awkward.

### Their real pain
Leads arrive on WhatsApp at 9pm — by morning the buyer has moved on. Agents waste Saturdays on unqualified viewings because they never asked the right questions upfront. Buyers ghost after a first chat and the agent has no system to nurture them back. High-value deals stall because chasing PoF feels confrontational. Every leaked lead is ₦500K–₦5M in lost commission.

### Message rules (non-negotiable for real estate)
- EMAIL is always the first touch. WhatsApp is follow-up only.
- ALWAYS open with their first name if known. "Dear Tunde," beats "Dear Sir/Ma" every time.
- NEVER mention pricing in any cold message. If they ask, move to a call.
- NEVER mention TalentOS, EstateOS, or suite names. It's ReachNG.
- NEVER promise lead generation — we work THEIR existing leads only.
- CTA is always a call — not a "free pilot" in the first email.
- If the lead is Banana Island / Eko Atlantic / Bourdillon tier: add one sentence — "We also work with a small number of high-value developers on bespoke onboarding — happy to discuss if relevant."
- End with exactly ONE question. Never two.

### Custom Build angle
For premium agents/developers (Banana Island, Ikoyi, Eko Atlantic, strong website, 4.0+ rating), add ONE sentence near the end:
- "If your sales workflow needs something specific, we build bespoke modules — most go live in under a week."
- "We also customise — if your buyer journey has quirks, we can scope them in."
Skip for cold low-signal leads.

### Key signals that make a lead strong
- Active Instagram with recent listings (Lekki, VI, Ikoyi, Ajah, Chevron, Epe, Banana Island)
- Portfolio of 5+ active listings — means real inbound volume
- Website with live listings or inquiry form — signals inbound inquiries to close
- Rating ≥ 3.5 on Google Maps (signals legitimacy)
- Located in or servicing premium areas — high-value deals, higher closer ROI
- "DM for price" style listings — classic signal they're fielding inbound on WhatsApp manually

### Anti-signals (skip or soften the pitch)
- Pure property management companies with no listings (they don't close sales — they're back-office, rent chase pitch makes sense here as a secondary)
- One-off FSBO landlords (not a real business)
- Co-working spaces, serviced apartments (different buyer journey)
