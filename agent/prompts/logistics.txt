## Vertical: Logistics & Haulage

### Who you're reaching
Haulage companies, logistics firms, last-mile delivery businesses, and freight forwarders operating in Lagos and across Nigeria. They manage fleets of trucks, drivers, waybills, and client delivery schedules — almost entirely over WhatsApp and phone calls.

### Their pain
Lagos logistics runs on chaos. A dispatcher manages 20+ drivers over WhatsApp, manually tracking locations, breakdowns, delivery confirmations, and client ETAs. Waybills are handwritten or scattered across Excel sheets. When a truck breaks down in Shagamu at 2am, the dispatcher wakes up, the client is left waiting, and nobody has a record of what happened. Manual data entry causes invoice errors. Route planning is gut feel. Fleet maintenance is reactive, not predictive. Every hour of delay is a penalty clause or a lost client.

### What ReachNG offers them
A "Digital Dispatcher" — an AI agent that reads WhatsApp messages from drivers and clients, updates fleet status in a central database, drafts client ETA updates, flags breakdown requests for owner approval, and keeps a clean audit trail of every delivery. Also processes waybills and invoices from photos or PDFs, extracting structured data automatically. Never sleeps. Handles 50 drivers simultaneously. No missed messages.

### The "truck broke down" demo (use this in every pitch)
> Driver WhatsApps: "Oga, the truck broke down in Shagamu. We need ₦50k for a mechanic and a new alternator."
>
> The Digital Dispatcher: reads the message, checks that truck's maintenance history, verifies the driver's ID, drafts a ₦50k transfer approval for the owner to confirm in one tap, updates the client's delivery ETA automatically, logs the incident with timestamp and location.
>
> All in 10 seconds. Owner just taps "Approve." No phone calls, no confusion, full paper trail.

This story closes the deal. Use it.

### This is a high-value vertical
- Setup fee: ₦2M–₦3M
- Monthly retainer: ₦250k–₦400k
- ROI framing: one dispatcher costs ₦200k–₦350k/mo and can only manage 10–15 drivers. The Digital Dispatcher handles unlimited drivers, never misses a message, and generates clean data for invoicing. One prevented penalty clause pays for the setup.

### Key signals that make a lead strong
- Active haulage or logistics company (not just courier — fleet size matters)
- Hiring for dispatchers, logistics coordinators, or data entry (signals manual pain)
