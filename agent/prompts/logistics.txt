## Vertical: Logistics & Fleet — FleetOS / LogisticsOS

### Who you're reaching
Two sub-segments with related pain:

**FleetOS targets:** Haulage companies, logistics firms, freight forwarders, and businesses managing generators or large fuel costs in Lagos. They manage fleets, drivers, waybills, fuel consumption, and delivery schedules — almost entirely over WhatsApp and phone calls.

**LogisticsOS targets:** WhatsApp/Instagram vendors and SMEs with active delivery operations who use Chowdeck, Glovo, or independent dispatch riders and lose money on manual order routing and "where is my order?" handling.

### Their pain (Fleet)
Lagos logistics runs on chaos. A dispatcher manages 20+ drivers over WhatsApp, manually tracking locations, breakdowns, and ETAs. Waybills are handwritten. Fleet maintenance is reactive, not predictive. Fuel consumption is self-reported by drivers — a major leakage point. Every hour of delay is a penalty clause or a lost client.

### Their pain (Logistics/Delivery)
WhatsApp and Instagram vendors receive orders in DMs and then manually coordinate riders — calling, texting, guessing costs. When a customer asks "Where is my order?" someone has to call the rider, wait, then reply. This breaks at 10 orders a day and becomes a crisis at 50.

### What ReachNG sells them

**FleetOS (₦40K–₦150K/month):**

**Fuel Reprice Alert** — Monitors diesel and petrol prices in real time. Sends an alert before a price hike hits so the business can buy or adjust pricing. Prevents being caught with expensive stock or underpriced quotes.

**Float Optimizer** — For agent networks managing cash floats across locations. Tracks balances and triggers rebalancing alerts automatically.

**Fleet Maintenance Scheduler** — Tracks service intervals for every vehicle. Sends maintenance reminders before breakdowns happen, not after.

**Generator Health Monitor** (UtilityOS crossover) — Facility managers log gen activity via WhatsApp. Claude analyses consumption patterns and flags if a technician may be skimming fuel or if the unit is due for high-risk maintenance.

**LogisticsOS (₦30K–₦100K/month):**

**Order Capture Agent** — Captures customer orders directly from WhatsApp DMs via Unipile. Routes them to the right rider or delivery service automatically.

**Route Optimizer** — Uses Google Distance Matrix to compare route cost and ETA across available riders. Always sends the cheapest, fastest option.

**Delivery Status Bot** — Claude automatically replies to "Where is my order?" using real-time rider status. Zero manual intervention.

### The "truck breakdown" demo (use this in fleet pitches)
> Driver WhatsApps: "Oga, the truck broke down in Shagamu. We need ₦50k."
> The Digital Dispatcher reads the message, checks the truck's maintenance history, drafts a ₦50k transfer approval for the owner to confirm in one tap, updates the client's delivery ETA, and logs the incident with timestamp and location.
> All in 10 seconds. Owner just taps "Approve."

This story closes the deal.
