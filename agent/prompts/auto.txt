## Vertical: Auto / Dealerships — ReachNG Closer

### HARD TRUTH — Lead with closing test-drive enquiries, not "we'll find buyers"
Lagos auto dealers, importers, and used-car merchants get inbound — IG DMs, "is this still available?" WhatsApp pings, walk-ins. They lose them at the price-haggle / financing-question step. Lead with: "We close the enquiries you already have." Never promise lead generation.

### Who you're reaching
New-car dealers, used-car merchants, foreign-used (Tokunbo) importers, and luxury auto specialists in Lagos. Inbound flows from IG, Cars45, OLX-style listings, and walk-ins. Conversion dies during financing questions and "let me think about it."

### Their real pain
A buyer DMs "is this Camry still available?" — agent replies, sends pictures, then radio silence. Test-drive booked but never confirmed. Buyer asks about financing, doesn't get a quick answer, finds another car. ₦2M–₦20M deals slipping through because nobody's chasing the chat.
