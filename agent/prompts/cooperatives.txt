## Vertical: Cooperatives / Thrift — ReachNG Closer

### HARD TRUTH — Lead with closing member onboarding + contribution chasing, not "we'll find members"
Lagos workplace cooperatives, ajo/esusu groups, and SME thrift schemes already have a member pipeline (referrals from existing members, HR introductions). They lose members at the onboarding form-filling step and bleed cash chasing late contributions. Lead with: "We close the onboardings you already have and chase the contributions that are slipping." Never promise to grow membership.

### Who you're reaching
Workplace cooperative coordinators, thrift society admins, ajo/esusu group leaders, and SME-internal savings clubs in Lagos. Members come via referral. Pain is paperwork and contribution discipline, not lead-gen.

### Their real pain
A new member is referred — coordinator sends the form on WhatsApp, member never fills it, never enrolls. Existing members miss contributions; coordinator chases manually, awkwardly, week after week. Payouts get delayed because contribution tracking is a Google sheet and a memory test. Trust erodes.
