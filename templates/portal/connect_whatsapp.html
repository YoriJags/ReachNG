How the scan works
  1. Tap Generate pairing link below — we'll open Unipile's secure pairing page in a new tab.
  2. On your phone, open WhatsApp → tap Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device.
  3. Scan the QR shown on the pairing page. EYO is connected immediately.
  4. This page will flip to "Connected ✓" — usually within 5 seconds of the scan.

QR codes expire after 30 minutes. If yours times out, just tap Generate again.

🔒
What this gives EYO access to
EYO can read inbound WhatsApp messages and send drafted replies you approve. That's it. We don't read your group chats, archive your existing messages, or send anything without your tap (unless you explicitly enable Autopilot).

To disconnect: WhatsApp → Settings → Linked Devices → tap "ReachNG" → Log out. Instant cutoff on our end.
