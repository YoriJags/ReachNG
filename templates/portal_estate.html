Getting Started
EstateOS · Tool guide for estate agents
Your portal is ready.
All tools below connect directly to your client account. Data is scoped to your company — no cross-client visibility.
What each tool does
My Properties
Add property listings with address, type, price, title type, and amenities. All listings are linked to your account and accessible for the Concierge tool. Use this as your live listing register.
Neighborhood Score
Enter any Lagos address and get an AI-generated score (0–100) covering: flood risk (based on known Lagos flood-prone zones), commute time to Victoria Island / Ikoyi / Ikeja at 8am peak traffic, and nearby amenity density (hospitals, schools, banks, supermarkets within 1.5km). Use this before listing to set the right price or before recommending a property to a buyer.
Property Concierge
Select one of your listings, then type a buyer's question. The AI reads your property details (address, price, title, description, amenities) and answers in the voice of a Lagos estate agent. Use this to quickly answer buyer FAQs — C of O questions, generator included?, flooding history, etc. — without needing to be physically present.
Proof of Funds
Before committing to a physical viewing, screen the buyer. Paste what they've submitted (bank statement summary, asset description, or their own words). The AI assesses: Can they actually afford the property? Are there authenticity red flags (round numbers only, no transaction history, suspicious formatting)? Returns PROCEED / REQUEST MORE INFO / DECLINE with a brief rationale. Protects your time.
KYC Vault
Paste any identity document text or description (NIN slip, international passport, utility bill, CAC certificate). The AI extracts structured data — full name, document number, issue/expiry dates, address, issuing authority — and flags any authenticity concerns. All extracted records are stored securely and linked to the party name. Use this to build your compliance vault before contract exchange.
Background Check
For landlords screening tenants before signing a lease. Enter the tenant's occupation, employer, income, rent amount, previous landlord feedback, and guarantor info. The AI calculates the rent-to-income ratio (recommended max: 33%) and gives a risk rating (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH) plus a recommendation (APPROVE / APPROVE WITH CONDITIONS / DECLINE) with specific flags for the Nigerian rental context.
Lawyer Bundle
When a deal is agreed, generate a formal handover memo for the closing lawyer. Enter both parties' names, agreed price, documents collected, and a brief negotiation summary. The AI produces a structured legal memo covering: transaction parties and roles, property details, document checklist, outstanding items before contract exchange, conditions for the deed, and next steps — all in Nigerian property law context (Land Use Act, Governor's Consent, C of O).
