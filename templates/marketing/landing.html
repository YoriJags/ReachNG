⚠ The safety rail

A bank-transfer screenshot is a workflow trigger, not a confirmation. Network issues, reversals, pending status, and outright fake screenshots all happen. ReachNG never sends a "confirmed" acknowledgment until you verify the funds in your account and tap approve. The default draft says "will confirm once it reflects". You can edit it upward to "confirmed" the moment you see the money. Your money, your verification, your tap.
