FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a free trial?

No free tier, but we do a 30-minute live demo on a real Lagos or Abuja dataset before you commit. You see exactly how the agent handles your vertical before paying a naira.

Do you take a cut of my revenue?

No. ReachNG never holds your funds. All deposits and payments go through your own Paystack account directly into your bank. We charge only the monthly SaaS fee.

Will my customers know they're talking to AI?

Only if you tell them. The agent writes in your tone, sends from your number, and asks you to approve every message. Most clients run it for weeks before any customer suspects.

What if WhatsApp bans my number?

We enforce a warmup ramp on all new numbers (10/day → 25 → 50). Plus we send within Unipile's compliance limits. The risk is far lower than blasting yourself.

Do I need any tech skills?

No. Setup is one 30-minute pairing call where we connect your WhatsApp via Unipile. After that you log in to your portal, approve drafts, and watch the pipeline.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime, take your data with you. We'll export everything (leads, threads, payments) to CSV and disconnect your WhatsApp pairing.
