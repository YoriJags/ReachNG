Built in Lagos

The AI sales engine WhatsApp-first businesses actually need.

Not another chatbot. Not another CRM. An agent that activates the leads you already have, follows up until they book or buy, and recovers the money already owed to you, so founders can do the work only they can do.

The problem we're attacking

Premium businesses don't lose money to volume. They lose it to inconsistency. A serious buyer asks a qualifier and gets a templated answer that breaks the spell. A referred prospect sends an enquiry while you're in a meeting and goes quiet before anyone replies. A staffer slips into the wrong tone on a high-ticket message, and a brand worth millions takes a hit nobody catches. The leads aren't missing. They're being handled by people who don't have the time, in a voice that isn't quite yours.

Off-the-shelf software was built for somewhere else. Stripe, not Paystack. English-language templates with no read on how a Nigerian buyer actually negotiates. Local tools tend to be a thin CRM or a bulk SMS sender; neither replies in your voice at midnight. And generic AI tools are confident, fast, and the first to embarrass a brand the moment they send something tone-deaf to a real customer.

ReachNG is the agentic employee for that gap. It reads inbound, drafts the reply in your house voice, parses transfer screenshots, qualifies enquiries, and never sends a single message you haven't tapped to approve. You stay in the seat that closes. The agent handles every minute before that.

Who's behind it

ReachNG is built by Oluyori Ajagun, a Lagos-based founder with a back-office and risk background. The product is opinionated because it's built by someone who's lived inside the operational pain it solves, not someone who read about it in a research deck.

Every line of code, every prompt, every piece of pricing logic is ours. Claude handles the language, Paystack handles the money, MongoDB Atlas handles the data. The agent runs on your own WhatsApp number, in your own voice, on infrastructure we own end to end.

What we believe

  • Activate, don't generate. You already have leads. We make sure none of them die quietly. Lead-gen is fragile and cold. Activation is universal and provable.
  • The agent does the work, the dashboard is just where the human approves it. We're not selling another SaaS to log into.
  • Trust is built one approved message at a time. Autopilot is earned, not defaulted.
  • Pain points beat verticals. If you're a Lagos or Abuja SME losing customers to slow inbound or money to slow follow-up, we serve you. Restaurant, law firm, beauty studio, it doesn't matter.
  • Naira-first, Paystack-paid. No dollar pricing. No "talk to sales" walls. No hidden fees.
  • Booked outcomes, not messages sent. The only metrics we report are calls booked, deposits collected and ₦ recovered.
