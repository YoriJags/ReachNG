No magic · Just three steps

How ReachNG actually works.

No mystery. No "AI black box." Three repeatable steps that take the work off your plate without taking control.

01

You already have leads. The agent picks them up.

Old WhatsApp chats. Dead spreadsheets from last year. Ignored IG DMs. Forgotten contact-form submissions. Referral handoffs. Live inbound today. Upload a CSV or connect your channels — every lead gets logged with full context (name, source, vertical, message) and routed to the agent. Nothing dies quietly anymore.

We integrate with Unipile (per-client WhatsApp), Meta Cloud API, custom webhooks for your forms, and BYO CSVs. The Lead Resurrection flow takes a 1,000-row spreadsheet and turns it into a live SDR queue in under an hour.

02

The agent drafts a reply that sounds like you.

Trained on a layered prompt — your business brief (your tone, never-say list, pricing rules) + your vertical's playbook (how Lagos restaurants speak vs how Lagos schools speak) + Nigerian market context (Paystack, Detty December, "send your account details") + the actual message. The draft is in your voice, in seconds.

Crucially: the agent does not send the message. It queues the draft for your approval. You can approve, edit, or skip — every single one.

03

You tap approve. It sends from your number.

One tap in your dashboard (or on your phone). The message goes out from your own WhatsApp number, with your branding intact. The customer thinks you typed it. Because you approved it, you might as well have.

As the customer replies back, the agent threads the conversation, drafts the next move, and queues again. Every step keeps you in control — every step saves you 90% of the typing.

After they sign — and before

Same agent recovers the money already owed to you.

The agent doesn't just close new deals — it chases the cash already promised. Unpaid invoices. Late rent. Outstanding school fees. Cooperative dues. Supplier credit. Same brain, same HITL approval, different playbook per debtor type. The "Recover Money" workflow runs alongside "Activate Leads" — every morning the Owner Brief tells you exactly how much cash is about to land and who needs a call today.

No extra fee. The cash workflows (Activate · Recover · Close · Retain) are the product — included in every plan.

Optional · Once you trust it

Flip on Autopilot for messages you'd always send anyway.

After a few weeks of approving drafts, you'll notice 80% are messages you'd send word-for-word. Flip the Autopilot toggle in your portal — the agent sends those low-risk replies directly. Anything risky (complaints, disputes, high-value events) still routes to your approval queue. Best of both.
