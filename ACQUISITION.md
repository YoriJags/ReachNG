## 5 · Channel-by-channel CAC math

| Channel | Cost per qualified visitor | Visitor → paid conv. | **CAC (paid client)** |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Founder DM (warm network) | ~₦0 | 25-40% | **~₦0** (time only) |
| Cold SDR funnel | ~₦300 | 1-2% | **₦15-30k** |
| Founder Twitter/LinkedIn organic | ~₦0 | 1.5% | **~₦0** |
| Paid Meta ads (Lagos premium) | ~₦300-600 | 1% | **₦30-60k** |
| Partnership referrals | rev-share 10-20% Y1 | 5-8% | **~₦60k** (1 month rev-share) |
| Client referral program | "free month" × ₦120k Growth | 100% (already qualified) | **₦120k** (cost of free month) |
| Conference / PR spikes | ₦500k / 100 leads = ₦5k each | 1.5% | **₦333k** (high) |

**Pattern:** warm founder network and Twitter/LinkedIn organic are the **two highest-leverage channels** for Y1. Paid ads scale volume but at 5-10× the CAC. Referrals are cheap *per client* but cap on existing client base. Conference spikes are expensive but high-trust.

**Implication:** Q1 is mostly free channels. Paid ads enter Q2 *only after* the funnel proves a 1.4% baseline conversion, otherwise we'd be burning naira on a leaky page.

## 7 · Risks ranked

### 1 · Yori-as-bottleneck on founder-led channels

All Q1 channels rest on the founder posting, DMing, replying. If Yori burns out or context-switches, traffic stalls. **Mitigation:** Q3 SDR hire takes outbound + half of organic content off founder. Until then, founder authority cadence is a daily 30-min ritual, time-boxed, non-negotiable.

### 2 · Meta ads don't convert at the assumed 1% on premium audience

Lagos B2B SaaS ads are still relatively unproven for premium SME tier (most Nigerian SaaS ads target retail/e-commerce). **Mitigation:** Q2 spend is small (₦450k total). Test 4 creative angles for 2 weeks each, kill losers fast. If full Q2 underperforms, redirect Q3 budget to referrals + partnerships.

### 3 · No published case study by end of Q1

The Y2 acceleration depends on social proof. If first paying client doesn't agree to a case study, Y2 traffic ramp slips by a quarter. **Mitigation:** make case study cooperation a soft requirement in the pilot pricing offer. "First 3 clients get founder pricing + case study collaboration."

### 4 · Founder authority cadence reaches no compounding audience

You post for 3 months, 50 followers, no inbound. Possible if topic-market mismatch or audience selection wrong. **Mitigation:** track follower growth + post engagement weekly. If at week 8 we're under 200 quality followers, pivot content angle (try operator narrative vs. tech-explainer vs. case-stat).

### 5 · Lagos premium SMEs trust no one new at this price

Selling ₦60-250k/mo to owners who haven't heard of you takes more time than any plan accommodates. **Mitigation:** the entire pilot pricing + founder pricing posture is designed to reduce this risk. First 3-5 clients close on founder-led trust + discount + case study collaboration.
